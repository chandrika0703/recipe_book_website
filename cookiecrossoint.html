CHOCOLATE COOKIE CROISSANT





History

From the heart of Paris, a delightful fusion has emerged that has set taste buds on fire and Instagram & TikTok FYP feeds ablaze. Introducing the viral sensation: the Crookie – a mouthwatering marriage of a flaky croissant and gooey cookie dough. Created by an ingenious bakery tucked away in the charming streets of Paris, this delectable treat has taken the world by storm, captivating dessert enthusiasts and foodies alike. Like most viral things, this recipe was created last year but has recently caught steam on our explore pages and feeds.A warm croissant, fresh out of the oven, with a surprise filling of soft, irresistible cookie dough–with a little extra cookie dough perfectly cooked on top. The contrast between the buttery, crispy layers of the croissant and the rich, sweet dough creates an explosion of flavors and textures that is nothing short of divine.

Ingredients

  • Hershey’s milk chocolate bars
  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • baking powder
  • granulated sugar
  • unsalted butter, room temperature
  • cream cheese, room temperature
  • vanilla
  • 1 egg with the yolk and white divided
  • sparkling or coarse sugar for topping

Method

Step-1:First, combine the dry ingredients in a large bowl.

Step-2:Next, beat together the butter, cream cheese, vanilla and egg yolk..

Step-3:Combine the dry ingredients with the cream cheese mixture.

Step-4:Turn the dough out onto a floured work surface.

Step-5:Roll into a 20 by 8-inch rectangle. Cut the dough into twenty 4 by 2-inch rectangles.

Step-6:Place 1 chocolate piece across the dough so the chocolate extends slightly over the edges. Fold the dough up and around the chocolate. Place the cookie, seam side down, on the prepared pan.

Step-7:Brush each cookie with a lightly beaten egg white. Sprinkle with coarse sugar and bake!

Step-8:Finally, drizzle each cookie with the remaining melted chocolate bar.

Step-9:Remove pie from oven and allow it to cool for about 15 minutes. Release it from the pan, slice and serve.
